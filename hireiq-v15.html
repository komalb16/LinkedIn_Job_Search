📅 Recommended Alert Schedules

⚡Every 30 min — Highly competitive roles (FAANG, hot startups). Be first to apply. Burns more API quota.
⏰Every hour — Active job search, mid-level roles. Good balance of speed and resource usage.
🕑Every 6 hours — Recommended default. Catches 95%+ of new postings. Works well for most roles.
🌙Daily — Passive search, senior/exec roles. Low urgency, maximum efficiency.
